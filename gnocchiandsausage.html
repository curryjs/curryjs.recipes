Gnocchi with Sausage Meatballs

Description

Thick and heart-warming meal of spicy italian sausage with gnocchi

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound spicy italian sausage
  • 1 tbs oil
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbs tomato paste
  • 14 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 pound gnocchi
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 oz mozzarella, grated
  • 3 tbs parmesan, grated

Steps

  1. Roll sausage into small meatballs, add to oil in a medium ozen proof pan at medium temperature
  2. Gently turn meatballs every 3 minutes or so until cooked through
  3. Cook gnocchi according to package instructions
  4. Add peppers and onions to meatballs and allow to soften
  5. Add garlic and once fragrant stir in tomato paste
  6. Pour in crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper, paprika, and mozzarella, stirring to combine
  7. Transfer finished gnocchi to pan and stir well to mix
  8. Top with parmesan and then put under broiler for a few minutes to melt
