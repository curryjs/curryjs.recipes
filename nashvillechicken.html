Nashville Hot Chicken

Description

A spicy fried chicken recipe

Chicken Brine and Flour Dredge Ingredients

  • 4 cups buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbs paprika
  • 6 to 8 pieces chicken
  • 6 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbs paprika

Hot Oil Sauce

  • 1/4 cup cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbs brown sugar
  • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tbs smoked paprika
  • 1 tbs chipotle chili powder
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. In large bowl, add buttermilk, hot sauce, salt, pepper, paprika, and whisk to combine. Then add chicken, insuring it is well covered, and refridgerate for up to 4 hours.
  2. Whisk together flour, salt, pepper, and paprika in another bowl to make dredge.
  3. Remove chicken pieces from buttermilk, allowing excess to drip off, then add one by one to flour dredge. Make sure each is well coated, shaking off excess flour. Put back in buttermilk and then into flour again before placing on a baking sheet or rack.
  4. Fill either deep fryer or large cast iron pan with oil and heat to 325
  5. Add 3 to 4 pieces at a time, cooking until golden brown and crisp on both sides for 12 to 14 minutes, flipping halfway.
  6. When all chicken pieces are finished combine oil ingredients in medium bowl, adding 1 cup of hot frying oil. Stir well to combine then drizzle or brush on cooked chicken pieces.
