Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Simple and Basic Stir Fry Recipe to start from

Ingredients

Stir Fry Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1/2 zucchini, sliced or cubed
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 small carrot, cubed
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 red pepper, sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cashews

Stir Fry Sauce Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chicken brother
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Steps

  1. Trim chicken thighs of excess fat and then cut into pieces
  2. Combine all ingredients for the sauce into a bowl and mix well
  3. In large pan or wok, on medium high heat, add 1 tbsp oil then add chicken once oil is heated
  4. Once chicken is cooked through, set aside. Add remaining oil and butter to pan and then add broccoli, zucchini, mushrooms, pepper, onion, and carrot. Cook until vegetables and crisp tender
  5. Add chicken back to pan, add garlic and ginger, cook until fragrant
  6. Add cashews then pour sauce over and bring to a boil. Turn heat down and let simmer until sauce is thickened
