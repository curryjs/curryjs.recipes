Pulled Pork Nachos

Description

Your favorite pulled pork nachos on top of a delicious bed of chips

Ingredients

  • Pre-made BBQ pulled pork
  • 1 bag tortilla Chips
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded monterey jack cheese
  • 1 diced tomato
  • sour cream
  • salsa
  • BBQ sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Assemble nachos in in layers in either cast-iron pan or other oven safe pan: chips, pork, onion, BBQ sauce, cheese, repeating as desired for multiple layers
  3. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbling
  4. Top with sour cream and salsa
