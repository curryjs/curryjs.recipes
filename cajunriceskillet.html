Cajun Rice Skillet

Description

Easy Cajun Sausage and Rice skillet for quick and delicious heat packed meal

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces Andouille Sausage
  • Olive Oil
  • 15 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 bellpepper, diced
  • 1 tsp paprike
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1.5 cups chicken broth
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Optional: 1 jalapeno
  • Optional: cheese

Steps

  1. Slice sausage, add sausage and oil to skillet or dutch oven and saute over medium heat until sausage is well browned
  2. Once sausage is browned, add bell pepper and cook for another minute
  3. Add the spices, stir and let roast for 1 minute
  4. Add tomatoes with juice, rice, chicken broth to skillet. Stir to combine
  5. Place lid on top, turn heat to medium-high to bring broth to a boil. Then turn heat to low and let simmer for 20 minutes
  6. After 20 minutes, remove from heat and leave lid on for additional 5 minutes
