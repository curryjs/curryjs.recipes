Tex-Mex Smothered Chicken

Description

Chicken Smothered in cheese

Ingredients

  • 12 oz boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 4 oz red enchilada sauce
  • 2 oz sour cream
  • 1 oz shredded cheddar-jack cheese
  • 1/4 oz cilantro
  • 2 tsp fajita seasoning

Steps

  1. Pat chicken breasts dry and season with fajita seasoning
  2. Place medium pan over medium heat and add 2 tsp olive oil, once heated, add chicken breasts
  3. Allow chicken breasts to cook through, about 5-7 minutes per side
  4. Once chicken is finished cooking, add enchilada sauce to hot pan and flip chicken until coated and sauce is bubbling, about 30-60 seconds
  5. Top chicken evenly with cheese, cover and cook until cheese is melted
