Stuffed Meatball Rigatoni

Description

Cheese stuffed meatballs, delicious baked rigatoni. Doesn't get much better than this!

Ingredients

  • 20 ounce ground beef
  • 12 ounce rigatoni pasta
  • 28 oz marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp tuscan heat spice
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Bring large pot of salted water to a boil and cook rigatoni according to package
  2. Soak half the panko with 3 tbsp water
  3. Add beef, half the tuscan spice, 1 1/2 tsp salt, and pepper to bowl with the breadcrumbs, then mix and seperate in to 8 3 inch wide rounds. Use half the mozzarella to stuff meatballs and then gently fold together
  4. Lightly oil baking sheet, spread meatballs out and bake on middle rack until cooked through, about 20-22 minutes, then remove from heat and turn broiler to high
  5. Once rigatoni is finished cooking, reserve 1 cup pasta water and then drain and set aside
  6. Melt 2 tbsp butter in microwave and then mix remaining panko, 1/2 tsp heat spice, salt and pepper
  7. Once meatballs and pasta are done, heat large oven-proof pan over medium high heat, stir in marinara, remianing spice, 3/4 cup reserved pasta water, 2 tsp sugar, 1 tsp salt. Bring to a simmer and cook until slightly thickened, 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in 2 tbsp butter. Generously season with salt and pepper
  8. Stir in drained rigatoni and meatballs. Top with parmesan, remaining mozzarella, and panko mixture. Broil until golden brown, 1-2 minutes
